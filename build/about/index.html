ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Julie.

I am a software engineer with a strong focus on user experience and front-end web development. I spend most of my time working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy making things usable and pretty (despite my lack of a formal design background).
I currently live in Pittsburgh, PA. I originally came here to get my BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh (CMU was far too expensive). I liked it so much that I decided to stay.
I currently share my home with Nikita, my rescued feline friend. She enjoys sleeping, eating, and helping me live up to my cat lady potential. I recommend the Animal Rescue League if you ever want an equally awesome cat friend of your own. Seriously, you want a cat in your life. Come over to the kitty side - we have fluffiness and meows!
In my free time, I enjoy indulging my cinephilia, geeky interests (science, skepticism, comics, video games, etc.), and love of cute animal pictures.
